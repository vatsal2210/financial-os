## Features

**Portfolio** — Total value, P&L, allocation breakdown, top movers with live prices

**Holdings** — Every position with FIFO cost basis, unrealized gains, daily change

**Transactions** — Searchable history with filters by account, type, symbol

**Watchlist** — Track symbols with price targets and topics of interest

**Feed** — Live price movers for everything you hold or watch

**Finances** — Income sources, expense tracking, monthly cashflow, savings rate

**Tax** — Canada and USA tax brackets, optimization checklists, receipt tracking

**Trading Rules** — Set your limits, get live compliance scoring

**Portfolio X-Ray** — Instant health grades: concentration, risk, discipline, returns

**Ask AI** — Natural language questions about your portfolio (bring your own Claude or OpenAI key)

---

## 100% Local

All data stored at `~/.financeos/` on your machine.

No cloud. No accounts. No telemetry. No tracking.

AI queries use your own API key. Stock prices from Yahoo Finance (public).
